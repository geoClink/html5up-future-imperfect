About Me

My background and passion

My Story

I’m a 2022 Colorado State University graduate in Agricultural Business, and I’m currently enrolled in the Apple Developer Academy. My path into product and development has been shaped by curiosity, people, and a love for practical problem-solving.

I grew up in Houston, studied in Fort Collins, spent a season in San Francisco, and now call Detroit home. Living in different cities taught me to notice how differently people move through spaces, systems, and technology.

I’m especially drawn to educational entertainment and digital experiences that feel clear, inviting, and intuitive. Outside of projects, I enjoy biking, visiting new cities, spending time with my parents, and trying new food at least once.

What I Build

I focus on front-end development, user experience, and accessibility-first design. I care about interfaces that are both polished and practical.

  • Semantic HTML, modern CSS, and JavaScript-driven interactions
  • Responsive layouts that adapt naturally across devices
  • Inclusive, WCAG-minded decisions from day one

What Guides Me

My approach is simple: make technology feel human. Accessibility, clarity, and thoughtful details are not extras; they are the work.

I believe great products begin with empathy, then follow through with clean, maintainable code.

I believe design should reduce friction and reveal order, not add noise.

I believe accessibility is a foundation, not a feature to tack on later.

I believe great software respects each device and meets people where they are.
