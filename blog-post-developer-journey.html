My Developer Journey: Growth & Lessons

Career Insights | March 2026

My path started in horticulture, moved through lab work, and grew into civic tech and iOS development. Each step taught me adaptability, user empathy, and the importance of continuous learning. Building apps for real users and collaborating with diverse teams has shaped my approach to product design and engineering. I believe in learning from every project and using feedback to improve.

  • Embrace change and new challenges
  • Focus on user needs
  • Learn from feedback and iteration
  • Celebrate milestones and growth
