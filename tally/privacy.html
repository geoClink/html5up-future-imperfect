Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 31, 2026

What Information Is Collected

  • Account authentication: Your email address is collected and stored via Supabase to create and manage your account.
  • Time tracking data: Sessions, clients, hours, dates, and task notes you enter are stored on Supabase servers.
  • Billing rates: Client billing rates you configure are stored on Supabase servers.
  • Payment processing: Invoices are generated through Stripe. Tally does not store your card or payment details — those are handled directly by Stripe.
  • In-app purchases: Purchase history for any in-app purchases is managed by Apple via StoreKit. Tally does not store this information independently.
  • Team workspace data: Workspace names, member email addresses, and member roles are stored on Supabase servers when you use team features.

What Is Not Collected

  • Tally does not use analytics or ad tracking of any kind.
  • Tally does not sell or share your data with third parties.
  • Tally does not collect device identifiers, location data, or behavioral data.

How Your Information Is Used

  • To authenticate you and provide access to your account.
  • To store and display your time tracking data, clients, and invoices.
  • To enable team collaboration features when applicable.

Third-Party Services

  • Supabase — database and authentication hosting. See Supabase Privacy Policy.
  • Stripe — payment processing for invoice generation. See Stripe Privacy Policy.
  • Apple / StoreKit — in-app purchase management. See Apple Privacy Policy.

Data Deletion

  • You can request deletion of your account and all associated data by emailing 1lclink2@att.net. Your data will be removed promptly.

Changes to This Policy

  • This policy may be updated from time to time. The effective date at the top of this page will reflect the most recent revision.
