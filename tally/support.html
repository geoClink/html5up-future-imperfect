Frequently Asked Questions

How do I delete my account?
Email 1lclink2@att.net with your request and we'll delete your account and all associated data promptly.
How do I restore my purchases?
Open Tally, go to Settings, and tap Restore Purchases. Make sure you're signed in to the Apple ID used for the original purchase.
