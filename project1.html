Stamped! — A City Passport

Accessibility-first redesign of a city passport e-commerce experience.

Overview

Stamped! is a city passport product that required an accessible, performant storefront. I led the front-end audit, refactor, and accessibility remediation to meet WCAG 2.1 AA standards.

Role

Front-end engineer — audit, implementation, QA, accessibility testing.

Challenge

Legacy markup and poor keyboard navigation were preventing some users from completing purchases.

Approach

  • Semantic HTML restructure
  • Keyboard and screen reader testing
  • Performance audits and lazy-loading images

Outcome

Improved conversion and accessibility; reduced page load time by 32% and remediated key WCAG issues.
