My Approach: From Idea to Launch

Process | March 2026

My process starts with user needs, moves through wireframing, prototyping, and iterative development, and ends with launch and feedback. I believe in rapid iteration, clear goals, and continuous improvement. Each project is a learning opportunity, and I strive to deliver products that solve real problems and delight users.

  • Start with user needs
  • Wireframe and prototype early
  • Iterate and refine
  • Launch, gather feedback, and improve
