Shipping MVPs That Matter

Product Thinking | March 2026

Launching a Minimum Viable Product (MVP) is about focusing on core value, iterating quickly, and listening to real users. For CommonSight, we started with essential features—reporting, mapping, and coalition tools—then expanded based on feedback. The key is to ship early, learn fast, and build what matters most. MVPs are not just about speed; they’re about validating ideas and creating a foundation for growth.

  • Identify the core problem and solution
  • Launch with essential features
  • Iterate based on user feedback
  • Scale thoughtfully
